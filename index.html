My SELF

I am a self-taught mobile photographer. My photographic adventure began in 2015 with quite an old-fashioned mobile camera. Because of difficulties in bad light conditions, the things weren't able to be achieved with the camera itself, so I had to solve them after, during the app's post-processing. I am a perfectionist with a sharp eye for details and a curious explorer looking for my own style. Regardless of naming, I simply want to be defined through the prism of my mobile photos because a day without a photo is a day lost.
